Dr. Jess Conser

AI Governance & Public Sector Transformation

Dr. Jess Conser is a Swiss‑American advisor specialising in the intersection of artificial intelligence, public policy, and institutional change. With a doctorate in public administration and a decade of experience advising national governments, she designs governance frameworks that enable responsible AI adoption at scale.

Her work has shaped digital transformation strategies for ministries across Europe and the Middle East, focusing on ethical AI, regulatory alignment, and citizen‑centric services. Dr. Conser is a sought‑after expert on AI assurance, helping public sector organisations move from experimentation to production with confidence.

At Power Consultancy, she leads engagements in AI governance, public sector digital strategy, and regulatory readiness — ensuring that our sovereign and institutional clients deploy AI that is both innovative and trustworthy.

Areas of expertise:

  • AI governance & ethics frameworks
  • Public sector digital transformation
  • Regulatory strategy & compliance
  • AI assurance & auditability
  • Cross‑jurisdictional policy
